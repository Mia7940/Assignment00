How To Get Into Reading


Step 1: Find Your Genre

If your someone who wants to get into reading but doesn’t know how or it just hasn’t been easy to get into this will help. First you want to find out what type of books you like (Ex. Thriller, Romance, or Fiction). You can do this by reading short stories with different genres and see which one has you wanting to read more.


Step 2: Find Your Starter Book

After you figure out what genre you can start looking for books. The best way to find out if a book with the genre you’re interested in is by reading different books descriptions and keep searching for one till you find one you’re interested in. Once you find the book that fits your interest give it multiple chapters to really grab your attention. Some books are slow burners, meaning it takes a while for the book to pick up. Repeat this process till you find a book that makes you want to read the next chapter. After you get your first taste of an interesting book, feed the interest with more books that have similar plots. Once you get deep into reeding you can start reading in many ways (Ex. Online, audiobooks).
